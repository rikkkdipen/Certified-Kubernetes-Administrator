SCHEDULER PROFILES — QUESTIONS & ANSWERS
========================================

1) Q: What are scheduler profiles?
   A: Multiple scheduling configurations inside one kube-scheduler process.

2) Q: Which field selects a scheduler profile?
   A: spec.schedulerName

3) Q: What happens if schedulerName is not specified?
   A: default-scheduler is used.

4) Q: Does schedulerName select a node?
   A: No, it selects which scheduler/profile handles the Pod.

5) Q: What does a scheduler profile contain?
   A: Plugin configuration and scheduling behavior.

6) Q: Where are scheduler profiles defined?
   A: In KubeSchedulerConfiguration file.

7) Q: What API version is used?
   A: kubescheduler.config.k8s.io/v1

8) Q: Can one scheduler process have multiple profiles?
   A: Yes.

9) Q: What must be unique in profiles?
   A: schedulerName

10) Q: What happens if schedulerName does not match any profile?
    A: Pod stays Pending.

11) Q: What are main scheduling steps?
    A: Filter → Score → Bind

12) Q: What is the role of Filter phase?
    A: Remove nodes that cannot run the Pod.

13) Q: What is the role of Score phase?
    A: Rank nodes to choose the best one.

14) Q: What is Bind phase?
    A: Assign Pod to selected node.

15) Q: Can profiles change scheduling behavior?
    A: Yes, by enabling/disabling plugins.

16) Q: What is addedAffinity?
    A: Extra node affinity added by scheduler profile.

17) Q: Does Pod affinity override profile affinity?
    A: No, both must be satisfied.

18) Q: What must be same across all profiles?
    A: queueSort plugin configuration.

19) Q: What is difference between profiles and multiple schedulers?
    A: Profiles = one process, multiple configs; schedulers = multiple processes.

20) Q: What command checks Pod scheduling?
    A: kubectl describe pod <pod>

21) Q: What indicates successful scheduling?
    A: PodScheduled = True

22) Q: Does scheduling success guarantee Pod running?
    A: No.

23) Q: What handles container startup?
    A: kubelet

24) Q: What are common errors after scheduling?
    A: ImagePullBackOff, CrashLoopBackOff

25) Q: Best one-line answer?
    A: Scheduler profiles allow multiple scheduling behaviors in one scheduler, selected by spec.schedulerName.
